Kitchen Table:

Building A Movement For This Moment

The Autonomous Tenants Union Network (ATUN-RSIA) is a collaborative of tenant unions in North America who have chosen to remain independent of:

  • Non-profits
  • Foundations
  • Government funding

in order to build power that led by and responsible to tenants.

Six years after the formation of the Autonomous Tenants Union Network, in early 2024 four militants from different unions began exchanging notes on their experiences as tenant organizers. Those four were organizers from the Crown Heights Tenants Union, the East of the Lake Local of Tenant and Neighborhood Councils (TANC) in the Bay Area, the Hollywood Local of L.A. Tenants Union (LATU), and the Sacramento Valley Tenants Union. In June of last year, we launched Kitchen Table as a monthly Zoom call for organizers in ATUN-associated tenant unions. In the beginning of 2025, a member of Puget Sound Tenants Union joined the Kitchen Table facilitation team.
