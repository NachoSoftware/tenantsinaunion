Kitchen Table:

Building A Movement For This Moment

The Autonomous Tenants Union Network (ATUN-RSIA) is a collaborative of tenant unions in North America who have chosen to remain independent of:

  • Non-profits
  • Foundations
  • Government funding

in order to build lasting, powerful instutions that are led by and responsible to tenants.

Six years after the formation of the Autonomous Tenants Union Network, in early 2024 four militants from different unions began exchanging notes on their experiences as tenant organizers. Their conversations developed into a monthly nation-wide call for organizers in ATUN-associated tenant unions that has been going on continually for over a year. Presently, the Kitchen Table organizing team represents:

  • The Crown Heights Tenants Union (CHTU)
  • Hollywood Local of L.A. Tenants Union (LATU)
  • The East of the Lake Local of Tenant and Neighborhood Councils (TANC) in the Bay Area
  • The Sacramento Valley Tenants Union (SVTU)
  • The Capitol Hill Local of the Puget Sound Tenants Union (PSTU)

As the only long standing place for radical tenant unions to discuss praxis, the reflections at Kitchen Table have produced a wealth of insight into the tenant movement that we try to share below.
